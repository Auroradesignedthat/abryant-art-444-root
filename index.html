Hi, My Name is Aurora

I create accessible, research-driven websites that help your business grow by turning your unique story into a clear, engaging online experience your customers will love.

What I Can Do For You:

I’m a web designer and qualitative researcher who believes that strong, research-driven design solutions empower businesses to sustainably grow and enrich the lives of their intended audience. My work blends scalable design solutions and creativity, with a commitment to inclusivity, social justice, and accessibility. If you’re ready to build something meaningful, I’m here to listen, understand your story, and design solutions that help your hard work thrive.
